\section{Homework question 1}

\begin{align*}
x-2)(x+4)=7 \\
x^2+2x-8=7 \\
x^2+2x-15=0 \\ 
(x+5)(x-3)=0 
\end{align*}
 Therefore $x = -5$ or $x = 3$
